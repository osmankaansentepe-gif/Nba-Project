Welcome To The NBA

The NBA is the most popular basketball league in the world. Millions of fans watch NBA games every season.

The league was founded in 1946 and today has 30 professional teams from the United States and Canada.

The NBA is famous for superstar players, amazing dunks, three-point shooting, strong rivalries and exciting playoff games.
