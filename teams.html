Popular NBA Teams

City: San Francisco | Arena: Chase Center | Championships: 7

Boston Celtics

The Celtics are one of the oldest and most historic teams in the NBA. They are famous for championship culture.

City: Boston | Arena: TD Garden | Championships: 18

Chicago Bulls

The Bulls became legendary in the 1990s with Michael Jordan and Scottie Pippen.
